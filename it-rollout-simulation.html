Overview

A strategic planning simulation for a hypothetical IT system deployment. [cite_start]The focus was on governance, documentation, and stakeholder communication[cite: 21].

Methodology

1. [cite_start]Scope Definition: mapped out all project requirements, dependencies, and critical path milestones[cite: 22].

2. [cite_start]Risk Management: Created a Risk Register to identify potential bottlenecks (technical & resource-based) and assigned mitigation strategies[cite: 22].

3. [cite_start]Reporting: Simulated "Stakeholder Updates" to practice clear, concise communication regarding project health and blocker resolution[cite: 24].
